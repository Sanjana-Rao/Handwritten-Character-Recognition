How to use the HCR tool:
  • Click on the 'Choose File' button and select the image
  • Click on the 'Submit' button and wait for the prediction
Things to keep in mind:
  • The image has to be in JPG format
  • The letter has to be drawn clearly on paper
  • Noise in the image may lead to incorrect prediction
  • This system has an accuracy of 95%
